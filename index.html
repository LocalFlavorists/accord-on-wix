In 4 different locations:

  • —A two-course meal: one savory and one sweet soufflé
  • —A generous apetizer: cheese & charcuterie board + a glass of natural wine
  • —A sweet treat: a slice of flan pâtissier
  • —And another sweet treat: madeleines

Once your booking is confirmed, you'll receive an email listing all 4 addresses on your food tour. This email also allows you to collect your Tour Kit, which includes your tasting vouchers and your local insider guide.

You can visit each venue whenever you like, as long as it's during their opening hours.

At your first stop (in any order you choose!), simply show your email to pick up your Tour Kit, then use your vouchers along the way.

This experience is designed around the original creations of our artisans and unfortunately cannot accommodate gluten-free, lactose-free, vegetarian, vegan diets, or nut allergies. To ensure a seamless experience for everyone, we recommend selecting another experience if you follow one of these diets.

Some dishes or products may contain pork. The charcuterie board can be made with beef instead, and the savory soufflé can also be adapted if needed.

Validity

You can complete your self-guided food tour across multiple days, and even multiple trips!

Vouchers can be used until December 31, 2026. Possible extension to January 2027, if you are traveling during the New Year period.

Availability

Our featured artisans welcome you throughout the year, except in August when many take their annual summer break.

On rare festive dates (such as Valentine's Day or New Year's Eve), a venue may be unavailable if it offers a special set menu that day.

If you're visiting Paris for the weekend, please note that not all products/dishes may be available, as two stops are closed on Sundays, something quite common in France. That said, the experience can be spread over two days depending on what's open.
